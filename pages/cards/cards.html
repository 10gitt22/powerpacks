Hero Cards
All cards of the "Getter" status are increase the amount of power cards in your inventory. The described properties will apply to all future PowerPacks NFT series.
CHANCE
No one knows the way, but his fate is in your hands.
It can transform into a "Getter" hero or disappear. When using the card - the player throws a dice, if successful: the card is replaced with a random hero's card, otherwise the card disappears.
  • Type:hero
  • Status:getter
  • Grade:silver
  • Rarity:rare
  • Series:1
GLADIATOR
Victory is often achieved not by force, but by will.
The holder of this card has the right to take part in fights in the arena. Finalists divide up valuable prizes. After the end of the event the card is returned to the owner's inventory.
  • Type:hero
  • Status:getter
  • Grade:silver
  • Rarity:rare
  • Series:1
The GLADIATOR card can be gathered from three components (you can get these components from packs or buy them on the market from other players):
BANKER
Never spend the Power not earned yet.
When in your inventory it adds +1 power daily (every 24 hours). The player is allowed exchange this card at any moment needed and get 1 power * number of days stored in the inventory. After the exchange, the player gets a chance to return that card into the inventory.
  • Type:hero
  • Status:getter
  • Grade:gold
  • Rarity:rare
  • Series:1
DUPLICATE
“A copy can't be better than the original!” I'll prove you otherwise.
Creates a copy of any power card in your inventory. When using this card, the player throws it away and the N amount of Power from the inventory, in return receives two N-Power cards. After the exchange, the player gets a chance to return that card into the inventory.
  • Type:hero
  • Status:getter
  • Grade:gold
  • Rarity:legendary
  • Series:1
ELDER
The wise one is not someone who knows much, but the one who knows the truth.
A 1% of Market Fee of the PowerPacks collection current sales is split between NFT data owners. The transfers occur once a week. Formula: 1% Market Fee/amount of NFT “ELDER”/168 Hours * N Hours of holding a particular NFT in your inventory.
  • Type:hero
  • Status:investor
  • Grade:platinum
  • Rarity:legendary
  • Series:1

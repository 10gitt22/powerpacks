MIRAGE
If you have nightmares, please contact me, I will help you make them come true.
The card copies the properties of any "bronze" or "silver" power card. In all events that will be associated with one of these power cards, "Mirage" automatically takes part. The first property of power cards also applies to this NFT, so the owner can put this card on the marketplace as a "250 power" at any time - it will be redeemed and burned.
  • Type:hero
  • Status:getter
  • Grade:gold
  • Rarity:rare
  • Series:2
BANKER
Never spend the Power not earned yet.
When in your inventory it adds +1 power daily (every 24 hours). At the moment of withdrawal points you can try to double your income (50% probability) - but this can destroy the NFT "Banker". If you take only: (1 power) * (number of days in inventory), the card stays with you forever. All card mechanics will work until the full game appears, after that happens - the "Banker" will no longer generate power points.
  • Type:hero
  • Status:getter
  • Grade:gold
  • Rarity:rare
  • Series:1
DUPLICATE
“A copy can't be better than the original!” I'll prove you otherwise.
Creates a copy of any power card in your inventory. If you have in your inventory for example "5, 25, 50, 250, 500, 1000 Power" and "Duplicate" - you can exchange the card "Duplicate" for any power card (of course the best way to do this with the "1000 power"). After the exchange, the player has a 15% chance to return the "Duplicate" card to his inventory.
  • Type:hero
  • Status:getter
  • Grade:gold
  • Rarity:legendary
  • Series:1
ELDER
The wise one is not someone who knows much, but the one who knows the truth.
1% Market Fee from current PowerPacks collection sales is split between NFTs owners. The transfers occur once a week. Formula: (1% Market Fee) / (amount of NFTs “ELDER”) / (168 hours) * (hours of holding a particular NFT in your inventory). This NFT has "Investor" status - it is limited. You can only get it from the Magma pack s.1.
  • Type:hero

  • Series:1

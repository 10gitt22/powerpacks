    What are NFTs?

    NFTs (Non Fungible Token) are unique crypto tokens that are managed on a blockchain. Thus, blockchain acts as the decentralized ledger that traces the ownership and transaction history of each NFT, which has a code and a unique ID, and other metadata that no other token can duplicate. Learn more: www.forbes.com/advisor/investing/nft-non-fungible-token/

    Our ultimate goal is to create a full-fledged economic role-playing tabletop fantasy strategy game based on the blockchain technology in a world where everyone will find
    what to do. Our team promises to make every effort to ensure that each stage is completed as soon as possible.

    In the first stage 4 series of PowerPacks will be launched, each series will have a unique Hero NFT and power cards of different denominations which you can immediately use on AtomicHub.

    Summer 2021

    Launching a full-fledged platform where you can take advantage of the functions of Hero cards. The second stage will be introduced gradually so you will be able to test the first abilities of the heroes much earlier.

    Autumn 2021

    The appearance of disaster events (these are adventures in which heroes with the “Lifeguard” status can participate only). Consider your set of heroes to choose where to go. Save settlers and creatures of different races and exchange them for NFTs with “Land” type.

    The release of the PowerP token - its introduction to the Powerpacks world.
